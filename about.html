About Me

Learn about my journey, qualifications, and approach to personal training

My Story

My journey into fitness began over 15 years ago when I first stepped into a kickboxing gym. What started as a hobby quickly became a passion that led me to compete professionally and eventually win two British kickboxing championships.

Throughout my athletic career, I developed a deep understanding of what it takes to achieve peak physical condition and mental resilience. After retiring from competition, I channeled this knowledge into helping others transform their lives through fitness.

My own experience with a lower back injury during my competitive years inspired me to specialize in this area, leading me to become a Level 4 Lower Back Pain specialist. I understand firsthand the challenges of overcoming physical limitations and use this experience to help clients who struggle with similar issues.

My Training Philosophy

The principles that guide my approach to personal training

Mindset

I believe that mental strength is just as important as physical strength. My training programs focus on building resilience, confidence, and a positive mindset that carries over into all aspects of life.

Fitness

Every body is unique, which is why I create personalized training programs tailored to individual goals, fitness levels, and preferences. My approach combines strength, mobility, and conditioning for balanced fitness.

Health

True fitness extends beyond the gym. I take a holistic approach that includes nutrition guidance, recovery strategies, and lifestyle adjustments to support overall health and long-term results.
