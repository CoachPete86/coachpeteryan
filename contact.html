Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services

How do I get started with personal training?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact me through the form above or give me a call. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your goals, assess your current fitness level, and create a personalized training plan. From there, we'll set up your first session and begin your fitness journey.

How often should I train to see results?

The frequency of training depends on your specific goals, current fitness level, and schedule. For most clients, I recommend 2-3 sessions per week for optimal results. Consistency is key in any fitness program, and we'll work together to create a schedule that fits your lifestyle while ensuring steady progress toward your goals.

Do you offer nutrition guidance?

Yes, nutrition is a crucial component of any fitness program. I offer nutrition coaching as a standalone service or as part of your personal training package. This includes personalized meal planning, macronutrient guidance, and ongoing support to help you make sustainable dietary changes that complement your training and accelerate your results.

Can you help with my lower back pain?

Absolutely. As a Level 4 Lower Back Pain specialist, I have extensive training and experience in helping clients overcome chronic back pain through targeted exercise and rehabilitation. My approach focuses on strengthening the core muscles that support your spine, improving mobility, and teaching proper movement patterns to prevent future injuries.

What should I bring to my first session?

For your first session, please bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a small towel. If you have any medical records or information about previous injuries that might be relevant to your training, please bring those as well. Most importantly, come with a positive attitude and readiness to begin your transformation journey!
