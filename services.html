How I Can Help You

As a professional personal trainer with extensive experience in competitive sports and specialized training, I offer a range of services designed to meet your unique fitness needs. Whether you're looking to improve your overall fitness, rehabilitate from an injury, or learn a new skill, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

All of my services include personalized programming, regular progress assessments, and ongoing support to ensure you achieve your goals efficiently and safely.

Lower Back Pain Rehabilitation

As a Level 4 Lower Back Pain specialist, I offer specialized training programs designed to alleviate lower back pain, improve mobility, and strengthen the core muscles that support your spine.

Having experienced lower back issues myself during my competitive career, I understand the frustration and limitations that come with this condition. My approach combines evidence-based techniques with practical experience to help you overcome pain and return to the activities you love.

What's Included:

  • Comprehensive assessment of your condition

  • Personalized rehabilitation program

  • Core strengthening and stabilization exercises

  • Mobility and flexibility training

  • Education on proper movement patterns
